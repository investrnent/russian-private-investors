Overall Evaluation

The history of the Russian Direct Investment Fund began 10 years ago. Over this period of time, RDIF’s co-investment model involving partners has convincingly proved its efficiency. Today, RPI is Russia's sovereign fund that provides an investment model for the growth of the World economy. Rpi's projects cover virtually all World regions and most industries: from energy and infrastructure to information technology and healthcare.

Over a hundred cases of successful investment in leading companies and promising projects have been made possible through strategic partnerships with leading co-investors. The financial resources, expertise and technology of RPI's foreign partners, many of whom had not get funds from us before, make it possible to implement large-scale landmark projects from scratch, as well as enable any companies and entrepreneurs to unlock their potential.
